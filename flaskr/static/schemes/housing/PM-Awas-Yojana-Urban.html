Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U)

Eligibility

Eligibility categories include EWS, LIG, MIG-1, MIG-2 and conditions like not owning a pucca house and satisfying other scheme rules.

Details / Benefits

Affordable housing through in-situ slum redevelopment, beneficiary-led construction, affordable housing in partnership, credit-linked subsidy components.

Application Process

Online application on PMAY-Urban portal (citizen assessment) or offline through implementing agencies; upload Aadhaar and required documents, save and print receipt on completion.
