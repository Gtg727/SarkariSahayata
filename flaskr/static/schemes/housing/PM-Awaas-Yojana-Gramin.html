Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G)

Details / Objective

PMAY-G provides a pucca house with basic amenities to houseless and those living in kutcha/dilapidated houses. Beneficiaries identified using SECC parameters and verified by Gram Sabhas.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance (example: ₹1,20,000 per unit for plain areas; higher for hilly/difficult areas).
  • Convergence with SBM-G, MGNREGA and other schemes for toilets, LPG, skilling etc.

Application Process & Documents

Apply via official PMAY portal or through local implementing agency per guidelines. Documents include Aadhaar, income proof, address, bank details, etc.
