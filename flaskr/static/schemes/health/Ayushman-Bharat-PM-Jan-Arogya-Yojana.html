Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)

Eligibility

Eligibility is based on SECC 2011 deprivation and occupational criteria for rural and urban families (bottom 40%).

Details / Objective

Large health assurance scheme providing cashless hospitalization cover of ₹5,00,000 per family per year for secondary & tertiary care to eligible families.

Benefits

  • Cashless coverage up to ₹5,00,000 per family per year (family floater).
  • Covers pre-hospitalization, hospitalization, medicines, diagnostics, ICU, implants, post hospitalisation follow-up (up to defined period).

Application Process

Identification via Arogya Mitra at hospital or CSC: search by name/ration card/mobile/URM etc; upload ID docs; SHA/insurer approves and issues e-card.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar / PAN / proof of address
  • Ration card (for family identification) and other supporting docs
