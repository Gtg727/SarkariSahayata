Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP)

Eligibility

Individuals or organizations can apply to open Janaushadhi Kendras; pharmacists (D.Pharma/B.Pharma) required for operation or must be employed by the Kendra.

Details / Objective

Scheme to provide quality generic medicines at affordable prices via Janaushadhi Kendras across the country.

Benefits

  • Substantial price reduction (50–90%) vs branded medicines.
  • Incentives for Kendra owners (normal & special incentives) and operating margins.

Application Process

Apply via official portal; register, upload documents, pay processing fee where applicable and follow the online process. Offline application guidance also provided by scheme portal.

Documents Required

Proof of pharmacist qualification, identity proofs, business space documents, bank statements, etc. (See official checklist).
