Grant of Transport Allowance to Differently Abled Persons

Overview

The Grant of Transport Allowance to Differently Abled Persons is a welfare scheme introduced by the Department of Social Welfare, Government of Puducherry. Under this scheme, a fixed transport allowance of ₹300/- per month is paid to eligible differently abled individuals through Anganwadi Centres.

Benefits

  • ₹300 per month as transport allowance.
  • Disbursed through Anganwadi Centres.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a native or resident of the Union Territory of Puducherry for at least 5 years.
  • Should have an orthopaedic disability of 40% or above.
  • Annual income should not exceed ₹75,000.
  • Should not receive any other financial assistance for conveyance from any source.
  • Should be 5 years or older.
  • Should not have a formal educational qualification.
  • If possessing a free bus pass, it should be surrendered to the Directorate of Social Welfare.

Application Process

Offline or Online (Print & Submit)

  1. Visit the Office/Sub-Office of the Department of Social Welfare during working hours OR take a printout of the prescribed application form.
  2. Fill in all mandatory fields in the application form, paste a passport-size photograph, and attach required documents (self-attested, if required).
  3. Submit the filled form to the appropriate authority based on your region:
    • Puducherry: The Deputy Director, Differently Abled Section, Directorate of Social Welfare
    • Karaikal: The Assistant Director, Social Welfare Department (Sub Office)
    • Mahe / Yanam: The Welfare Officer (i/c), Social Welfare Department (Sub Office)
  4. Request an acknowledgment or receipt after submission. Ensure it includes date, time, and unique ID if applicable.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Residence-cum-Nativity Certificate (from Competent Authority)
  • Recent Passport-size Photograph
  • Bank Passbook
  • Disability Certificate / Medical Certificate
  • Community Certificate
  • Financial Assistance ID Card of deceased (if applicable)
  • Electoral Voter Identity Card
  • Declaration Form

Note:

This scheme is implemented by the State of Puducherry under the Department of Social Welfare.
