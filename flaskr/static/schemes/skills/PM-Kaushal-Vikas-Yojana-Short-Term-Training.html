Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)

Details

Short Term Training (STT) at PMKVY training centres, training under NSQF, soft skills, entrepreneurship and placement assistance on successful assessment.

Eligibility

Indian nationals aged approx. 15-45 (varies by job role), Aadhaar required for enrolment.

Benefits & Support

  • Training, digital content, counselling, boarding/lodging/transport support in many cases, insurance, incentives on certification.

Application

Contact nearest PMKVY training center; apply per centre instructions.
