Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)

Details

Credit-linked subsidy scheme administered by MSME to create employment through setting up micro-enterprises in rural & urban areas.

Benefits

Subsidy and credit-linked assistance; objective to create large-scale employment and encourage self-employment.

Application

Apply through district level PMEGP offices / online portal as per scheme instructions.
