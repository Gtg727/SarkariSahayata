Free Education for Sports Medal Winners / Participants

Details & Benefits

Financial assistance for tuition/hostel and allowances for medal winners/participants in national/international events; aims to support medal-winning sportspersons pursuing education.

Eligibility & Documents

Medal winners at recognized events as per scheme rules; required proof of awards, IDs, academic admission documents, etc.
