Savitribai Jyotirao Phule Fellowship for Single Girl Child

Overview

The Savitribai Jyotirao Phule Fellowship for Single Girl Child (SJSGC) is a UGC initiative for single girl children pursuing a Ph.D. in recognized institutions. It aims to encourage higher education for girls and promote the single girl child norm in society. Twin or fraternal daughters without any other siblings are also eligible.

Objectives

  • To support higher education of single girl children in Social Sciences.
  • To recognize and promote the small family norm.
  • To promote and support single girl children in society.

Benefits

Tenure

Fellowship is provided for a maximum of 5 years, effective from 1st April of the selection year or from the date of joining.

Financial Assistance

  • JRF: ₹31,000 per month (first 2 years)
  • SRF: ₹35,000 per month (remaining tenure)
  • Contingency (Humanities & Social Sciences): ₹10,000 p.a. (first 2 years), ₹20,500 p.a. (rest)
  • Contingency (Science & Tech.): ₹12,000 p.a. (first 2 years), ₹25,000 p.a. (rest)
  • Escort/Reader Assistance (Divyang): ₹3,000 p.m.

HRA (House Rent Allowance)

  • Hostel stay: Eligible only for hostel fees (excludes mess, electricity, etc.)
  • If hostel not available: Rent reimbursed as per GoI norms.
  • Own accommodation: HRA applicable as per GoI city category norms.

Medical Assistance

No separate provision, but institutional medical facilities can be availed.

Leave

  • 30 days earned leave per year + public holidays
  • Maternity/paternity leave as per GoI norms (once during tenure)
  • Intermittent break for women scholars up to 1 year (max 3 times, no fellowship during breaks)
  • Academic leave (max 1 year, unpaid, included in tenure)

Eligibility

  • Must be a single girl child of her parents (no siblings).
  • Must be pursuing full-time Ph.D. (regular mode) in a recognized institute.
  • Age limit: 40 years (General), 45 years (SC/ST/OBC/PwD) at time of application.

Exclusions

  • If the girl has any siblings (male or female), she is not eligible.
  • Part-time/distance/open education Ph.D. courses are not eligible.

Application Process (Online)

  1. Visit https://frg.ugc.ac.in/
  2. Click “New User” to register for the relevant scheme.
  3. Fill in registration details and upload scanned photo and signature (Max: 1MB, JPG format).
  4. Receive login credentials via email, log in and change the password.
  5. Complete profile and eligibility form. Upload affidavit of ₹100 on stamp paper as proof of being a single girl child (attested by SDM/Magistrate).
  6. Fill PG and Ph.D. details in the Data Capturing section.
  7. Upload required documents including research proposal and signed application form.
  8. Agree to the declaration and submit the form.

Note: No hard copy of the application is accepted at any stage.

Documents Required

  • Scanned passport-size photograph and signature (Max 1MB, JPG format)
  • Complete research proposal (Max 5MB) and abstract (Max 1MB)
  • Signed application form (by HoD/Registrar)
  • Affidavit of ₹100 stamp paper confirming single girl child status, attested by SDM/First Class Magistrate/Tahsildar (Max 1MB)
