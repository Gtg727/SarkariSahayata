Prime Minister’s Fellowship for Doctoral Research

Eligibility

Indian citizen; full-time PhD scholar in recognized Indian institution; PhD registration date not more than 14 months from application; valid industry partner for mentorship and co-funding.

Details / Objective

Fellowship by SERB (with implementing partner FICCI/CII) to encourage industry-relevant research; provides scholarship and mentorship to promote commercialization of R&D.

Benefits

Enhanced fellowship amount (beyond JRF/SRF), national/international exposure, mentorship and resources to support industry-relevant research.

Application Process

Apply online through the scheme portal (specified in guidelines), prepare proposal, tie-up with industry partner and submit required documents as per portal instructions.

Documents Required

  • PhD enrolment & registration certificate
  • Academic certificates, research synopsis, CVs of supervisors and industry partner documents
