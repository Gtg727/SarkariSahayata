Prime Minister Early Career Research Grant

Eligibility

Indian citizens (with some provisions for OCI/PIO), PhD degree required; applicant in regular academic/research position; certain age and experience limits apply.

Details / Objective

ANRF/Department of Science & Technology program to support early career researchers with a flexible research grant to help them start independent research careers.

Benefits

Research grant (examples: up to multiple lakhs over 3 years), support for personnel, equipment, travel, overheads and publication/patent costs.

Application Process & Documents

Register and apply via the ANRF portal; upload technical document and supporting templates; selection by expert committee. Documents include bio-data, endorsement letters, age proof, plagiarism undertaking, etc.
