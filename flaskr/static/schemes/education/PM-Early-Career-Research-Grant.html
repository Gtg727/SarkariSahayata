Prime Minister Early Career Research Grant

Education Research Funding

Eligibility

Indian citizens (with some provisions for OCI/PIO). Applicants must hold a PhD degree and be in a regular academic or research position. Age and experience limits apply as per ANRF norms.

Details / Objective

An ANRF / Department of Science & Technology initiative to support early-career researchers with flexible funding, enabling them to establish independent research programs.

Benefits

  • Research grant covering multiple lakhs over 3 years
  • Support for research personnel
  • Funding for equipment & consumables
  • Travel, overheads, and publication/patent expenses

Application Process

Applications must be submitted through the ANRF portal along with the required technical documents. Proposals are evaluated by an expert selection committee.

📄 Documents Required

  • Bio-data / Curriculum Vitae
  • PhD Degree Certificate
  • Proof of Age
  • Institutional Endorsement Letter
  • Detailed Research Proposal
  • Plagiarism & Originality Undertaking
  • Additional ANRF Prescribed Formats
