Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA)

Details

Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) was launched on 24th April 2018 'National Panchayat Day' by the Hon'ble Prime Minister. It aims to develop and strengthen the Panchayati Raj System across India in rural areas.

The scheme strengthens rural local governance to make Panchayats more responsive to local development needs and achieve sustainable solutions aligned with SDGs.

Objectives

  • Develop governance capabilities of Panchayati Raj Institutions (PRIs) to deliver on SDGs.
  • Enhance Panchayat capacity for inclusive local governance and resource utilization.
  • Promote e-governance and good governance in Panchayats.
  • Strengthen Gram Sabhas for transparency and accountability.

Benefits

  • Capacity Building and Training (CB&T)
  • Use of IT for Distance Learning and e-Enablement
  • Support for Gram Panchayat Infrastructure
  • Handholding support for GPDP Formulation
  • Technical and financial assistance to PRIs

Eligibility

Panchayati Raj Institutions of all States/UTs are eligible, including rural local government institutions where Panchayats do not exist.

Application Process

Online: Register on the RGSA portal - gramswaraj.nirdpr.in

  1. Click on Register.
  2. Fill in the required details.
  3. Submit and track your application.

Documents Required

  • Annual Action Plan
  • Utilization Certificate
  • Audit Report
  • Physical & Financial Progress Reports
