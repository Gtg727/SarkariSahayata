Development Support for Coffee in Traditional Areas: Water Augmentation

Coffee | DBT | Farmer | Subsidy | Water

Details

The scheme “Development Support for Coffee in Traditional Areas: Water Augmentation” is a sub-component of the Integrated Coffee Development Project during the Medium Term Framework (MTF) by the Coffee Board, Department of Commerce, Ministry of Commerce and Industry. The objective is to enhance production and productivity of coffee plantations through water augmentation in traditional coffee-growing regions.

Benefits

Scale of Subsidy

40% of the unit cost, with a ceiling of ₹2,50,000 per beneficiary for all the components under the water augmentation scheme.

Additional Support for SC/ST Growers

Coffee growers belonging to SC/ST communities with coffee holdings up to 4 hectares are eligible for an extra 10% subsidy (total ceiling ₹2,50,000).

Mode of Payment

Subsidy amount is transferred directly to the applicant’s bank account through EFT, RTGS, or NEFT after approval by the Deputy Director (Extn.).

Eligibility

  • Individual growers, joint owners, or family members applying jointly (mother, father, wife, and children).
  • Applicants must possess coffee holdings up to 10 hectares.
  • Applicable for water harvesting structures and irrigation equipment.
  • Beneficiaries cannot reapply for the same component during the MTF period.
  • Growers who availed subsidy for the same activity in the past 10 years are not eligible again until the 11th year.
  • Infrastructure should be located within or near the coffee estate.
  • Non-traditional area coffee growers (non-tribal) with holdings up to 10 hectares are eligible.

Application Process

Online – For Feasibility Report

  1. Visit the Coffee Board Official Website.
  2. Click on “Online Services” → “Subsidy” to access the form.
  3. Fill in the form and upload required documents.
  4. Review and submit the form after agreeing to the terms.
  5. Your local Coffee Board office will review and inspect your application.
  6. A Technical Feasibility Report (TFR) will be issued after inspection.
  7. Note: Activities started before obtaining TFR are not eligible for subsidy.

For Claiming Subsidy

  1. Submit the subsidy claim form with documents to your jurisdictional Coffee Board office.
  2. Documents are scrutinized and a field inspection is conducted.
  3. A Physical Verification Report is prepared and verified.
  4. Deputy Director (Extn.) approves and releases the subsidy to your bank account.

Documents Required

For Feasibility Report

  • Duly filled and signed application and self-declaration (duplicate).
  • Aadhaar Card (UIDAI).
  • Proof of land ownership (Patta, RTC, Chitta, Adangal, or Land Possession Certificate).
  • Sketch of estate with proposed activity site marked.
  • Quotation for machinery/equipment.
  • Plan and estimation for water structures (tank/well).
  • Registered GPA (if applicable).
  • SC/ST caste certificate (if applicable).
  • Copy of bank passbook (Aadhaar seeded preferred).

For Claiming Subsidy

  • Claim statement in prescribed format (duplicate).
  • Original invoice/bill with TIN for equipment purchase.
  • Work completion and fund utilization report signed by applicant.
