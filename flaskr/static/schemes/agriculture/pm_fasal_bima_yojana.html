Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Agriculture | Crop Insurance | Farmers | Risk Management

Details

The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) was launched on 13th January 2016 by the Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, Government of India. It aims to provide comprehensive risk insurance coverage to farmers against crop loss due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases, ensuring financial stability and encouraging sustainable agriculture.

Objectives

  • Provide insurance coverage and financial support to farmers in case of crop failure.
  • Stabilize the income of farmers to ensure their continuity in farming.
  • Encourage farmers to adopt modern agricultural practices.
  • Ensure flow of credit to the agriculture sector.

Benefits

  • Farmers pay a nominal premium — 2% for Kharif crops, 1.5% for Rabi crops, and 5% for commercial/horticultural crops.
  • Remaining premium is subsidized by the government.
  • Coverage for losses due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases.
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to farmers’ bank accounts.

Eligibility

  • All farmers growing notified crops in notified areas are eligible.
  • Both loanee (with crop loan) and non-loanee farmers can apply.
  • Farmers must have valid land records and bank account details.

Application Process

  1. Farmers can enroll through their bank branches, Common Service Centres (CSCs), or online via pmfby.gov.in.
  2. Provide crop details, land ownership proof, Aadhaar number, and bank details.
  3. Pay the required premium amount and collect the acknowledgment receipt.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Land Ownership Proof
  • Bank Account Details
  • Crop Sowing Certificate (if applicable)
  • Recent Passport Size Photograph
