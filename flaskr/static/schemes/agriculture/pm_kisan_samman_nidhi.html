Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Agriculture | Farmers | Direct Benefit Transfer

Details

The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) is a Central Sector Scheme launched by the Government of India in February 2019 to provide income support to all landholding farmer families in the country to supplement their financial needs for procuring various inputs related to agriculture and allied activities.

Objectives

  • Provide financial support to all eligible landholding farmer families.
  • Ensure timely income to meet agricultural input and household needs.
  • Reduce farmers’ dependence on informal credit sources.

Benefits

  • ₹6,000 per year in three equal installments of ₹2,000 each.
  • Funds directly transferred to beneficiaries’ bank accounts via DBT.
  • Helps farmers in purchasing seeds, fertilizers, and other inputs.

Eligibility

  • Small and marginal farmers owning cultivable land are eligible.
  • Institutional landholders and higher-income farmers are excluded.
  • Applicants must have valid land records and Aadhaar linkage.

Application Process

  1. Farmers can apply at the local revenue office, CSC centers, or online through the PM-KISAN portal.
  2. Submit required documents like Aadhaar, bank passbook, and landholding certificate.
  3. Applications are verified by state authorities before approval.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Account Details
  • Land Ownership Proof / Record of Rights
  • Address Proof
  • Recent Passport Size Photograph
