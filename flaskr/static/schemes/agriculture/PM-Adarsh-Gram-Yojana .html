Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAGY)

Eligibility

Primarily SC-majority villages (≥50% SC), recognized in latest Census; villages with ≥40% SC included in some states; typically villages with population ≥500 considered.

Details / Objective

PMAGY aims integrated development of SC majority villages through convergent implementation of Central & State schemes and ‘gap-filling’ funds of up to ₹20 lakh per village for activities not covered by other schemes.

Benefits

  • Convergent implementation of schemes (PMAY-G, MGNREGS, Ujjwala, SBM etc.).
  • Gap-filling central assistance (₹20 lakh per village).
  • Infrastructure and social empowerment through Village Development Plan (VDP).

Application / Implementation

Offline: Village profile submissions, household surveys, preparing/approving VDP, implementation and progress reporting to district authorities. Villages declared ‘Adarsh Gram’ after meeting criteria.

Documents / Inputs

Village Development Plan, committee details, baseline survey outputs, convergence plan and supporting documents as per scheme formats.
