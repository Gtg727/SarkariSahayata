National Livestock Mission: Establishment of State Semen Bank

Details

The scheme aims to improve sheep and goat breeds through selective breeding using artificial insemination. It provides support for strengthening state semen banks and enhancing animal productivity.

Benefits

  • One-time assistance of ₹10,00,000 for strengthening semen banks.
  • Funds for equipment and cryo-containers for storing goat frozen semen.

Eligibility

Eligible entities include State Livestock Agencies and Animal Husbandry Departments.

Application Process

  1. Visit the National Livestock Mission website.
  2. Register and log in as Government/Agency/Entrepreneur.
  3. Fill in details and upload necessary documents.
  4. Submit the application for review.

Documents Required

  • Detailed Project Report (DPR)
  • Land and ownership documents
  • PAN, GST, and incorporation certificates
  • Bank statements and canceled cheque
  • Photographs and address proof
