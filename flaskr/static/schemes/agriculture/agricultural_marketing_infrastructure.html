Agricultural Marketing Infrastructure (AMI)

Details

AMI aims to develop storage and marketing infrastructure for farmers to enhance income and reduce post-harvest losses. It provides subsidies to individuals, FPOs, cooperatives, and entrepreneurs.

Benefits

  • Remunerative prices for produce
  • Reduce post-harvest losses
  • Promote small processing units
  • Support farmer-consumer linkages

Eligibility

Individuals, FPOs, SHGs, Cooperatives, and State agencies are eligible.

Application Process

Offline:

  1. Apply for Term Loan to Financial Institution (FI).
  2. FI applies for Advance Subsidy through NABARD ENSURE portal.
  3. Project completion and final subsidy disbursal after inspection.

Documents Required

  • Project report and technical appraisal
  • Loan sanction letter
  • Land ownership documents
  • Partnership deed / Incorporation certificate
  • Affidavit and promoter category certificate
