Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Credit Card | Kisan | Agriculture | Loan

Details

The Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme was introduced to provide adequate and timely credit to farmers for their agricultural operations. The Government of India offers an interest subvention of 2% and a Prompt Repayment Incentive of 3%, making credit available at a subsidized rate of 4% per annum. The scheme was further expanded in 2004 to include allied and non-farm activities and updated in 2012 for electronic Kisan Credit Cards.

Objectives / Purpose

  • Provide timely credit support under a single window for agricultural needs.
  • Meet short-term crop cultivation credit requirements.
  • Cover post-harvest expenses and produce marketing loans.
  • Support consumption and working capital needs of farm households.
  • Provide investment credit for allied and non-farm activities.

Type of Card

  • Magnetic stripe card with PIN and ISO IIN for ATM and micro ATM access.
  • Option for biometric authentication using Aadhaar.
  • EMV and RuPay compliant smart cards with magnetic stripes and PIN.
  • Cards can transact with dealers and receive sales proceeds from mandis and procurement centers.

Delivery Channels

  • Withdrawals through ATMs / Micro ATMs.
  • Transactions via Business Correspondents (BCs) using smart cards.
  • Point-of-Sale (PoS) machines with input dealers.
  • Mobile Banking with IMPS / IVR facilities.
  • Aadhaar-enabled payment systems.

Benefits

  • Fixation of credit limit based on scale of finance, area cultivated, and insurance costs.
  • Increased limit annually by 10% for inflation and cost escalation.
  • Term loans for land development, irrigation, and farm equipment.
  • Separate sub-limits for short-term and term loans for operational convenience.
  • Collateral-free loans up to the prescribed limit based on bank policies.

Eligibility

  • Individual or joint farmers who are owner cultivators.
  • Tenant farmers, oral lessees, and sharecroppers.
  • Self Help Groups (SHGs) or Joint Liability Groups (JLGs) of farmers.

Application Process

Online

  1. Visit the website of the bank offering the Kisan Credit Card scheme.
  2. Select the Kisan Credit Card option and click on “Apply”.
  3. Fill in all required details and submit the application form.
  4. Note the application reference number for tracking.
  5. If eligible, the bank will contact you within 3–4 working days for further processing.

Offline

  1. Visit your nearest bank branch offering the Kisan Credit Card scheme.
  2. Collect and fill out the KCC application form.
  3. Attach necessary documents and submit the form to the bank officer.
  4. Await verification and card issuance after approval.

Documents Required

  • Duly filled application form.
  • Two passport-size photographs.
  • Valid ID proof – Aadhaar, Voter ID, Passport, or Driving License.
  • Address proof – Aadhaar, Driving License, or similar documents.
  • Proof of landholding certified by revenue authorities.
  • Details of cropping pattern (crops grown and acreage).
  • Security documents for loan limit above ₹1.6–3 lakhs, as applicable.
  • Any additional documents as required by the bank.
