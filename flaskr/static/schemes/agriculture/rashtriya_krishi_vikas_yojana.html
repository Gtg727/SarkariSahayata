Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY)

Agriculture | Farmers | Development | Innovation

Details

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) is a centrally sponsored scheme launched by the Government of India in 2007 under the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare. The aim is to incentivize states to increase public investment in agriculture and allied sectors and to ensure holistic development of the agricultural economy.

Objectives

  • Ensure holistic development of agriculture and allied sectors.
  • Encourage states to invest more in the agricultural sector.
  • Increase farmers’ income through modern technology and innovation.
  • Promote entrepreneurship and agribusiness startups among rural youth.
  • Strengthen the infrastructure needed for post-harvest management and marketing.

Key Components

  • RKVY-RAFTAAR: Focuses on Agri-Innovation, Agri-Entrepreneurship, and Startups.
  • Infrastructure and Asset Development: Support for irrigation, storage, and processing facilities.
  • Production Growth: Promotion of high-yield and climate-resilient crop varieties.
  • Value Chain Development: Linking farmers to markets and improving supply chains.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance for infrastructure creation in the agricultural sector.
  • Support for agri-entrepreneurs and startups in agriculture and allied activities.
  • Capacity building and skill training for farmers and rural youth.
  • Improved productivity and market access for farmers.

Eligibility

  • All Indian citizens engaged in farming, agribusiness, or allied activities are eligible.
  • Startups and entrepreneurs working in agriculture can apply under RKVY-RAFTAAR.
  • Farmers’ Producer Organizations (FPOs), Self Help Groups (SHGs), and Cooperatives can also apply.

Application Process

  1. Interested farmers and entrepreneurs can apply through their State Agriculture Department or RKVY portal.
  2. Submit the required documents along with the project proposal (if applicable).
  3. After review and approval, beneficiaries receive financial support directly into their bank accounts.
