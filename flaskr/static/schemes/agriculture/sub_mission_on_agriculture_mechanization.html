Sub-Mission On Agriculture Mechanization (SMAM)

Details

To promote the usage of farm mechanization and increase productivity, the SMAM scheme provides financial assistance for purchasing agricultural machinery and establishing custom hiring centers across India.

Mission Strategy

  • Performance testing of farm machinery.
  • Promote mechanization through training and demonstrations.
  • Provide financial assistance for machinery procurement.
  • Establish Custom Hiring Centres for farm equipment.

Benefits

  • Subsidy from 50% to 80% on agricultural machinery.
  • Special priority to women farmers.
  • Increase yield and reduce cost of production.
  • Helps small & marginal farmers access modern equipment.

Eligibility

All landholding farmers, SHGs, FPOs, and entrepreneurs are eligible. Priority is given to financially weak and women farmers.

Application Process

Online: Apply on agrimachinery.nic.in

  1. Register on the portal.
  2. Fill required details and upload documents.
  3. Submit the form and track your application.

Documents Required

  • Aadhar card
  • Land ownership proof
  • Bank passbook copy
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size photo
