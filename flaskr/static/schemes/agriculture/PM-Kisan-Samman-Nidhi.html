Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Eligibility

All landholding farmers' families which have cultivable land holding in their names are eligible to get benefit under the scheme.

Details / Objective

The scheme aims to supplement the financial needs of all landholding farmers’ families by providing cash support to procure various inputs to ensure proper crop health and appropriate yields and for domestic needs. Under the scheme an amount of ₹6,000 per year is released online directly into bank accounts by Direct Benefit Transfer mode.

Benefits

  • Financial benefit of ₹6,000 per annum per family payable in three equal installments of ₹2,000 each.

Exclusions

Certain categories such as institutional land holders, current/former constitutional post holders, higher-income professionals, pensioners with high pensions, income-tax payers etc. are excluded. Refer official guidelines for full list.

Application Process

Online via Common Service Centers (CSC). VLE fills farmer registration, land details and uploads supporting documents; payment and status checks follow as per CSC workflow.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Landholding papers
  • Savings Bank Account details
